ACFUSA Elvanto Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 29, 2025

At African Christian Fellowship USA (ACFUSA) and its associated regions and chapters, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our members, volunteers, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal information through our Elvanto platform (also referred to as "ACF Online").

What Information We Collect

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address (optional)
  • Marital and family relationships
  • Chapter and group affiliations
  • Event attendance
  • Volunteer assignments
  • Donation records (if connected to Tithely Giving)

Why We Collect It

We use this information to:

  • Manage membership records
  • Organize and track event participation
  • Communicate with members
  • Coordinate volunteer efforts
  • Provide pastoral and community care
  • Track giving (when applicable)
  • Enhance engagement across chapters and regions

Who Can See This Information

Access to personal data is limited to:

  • Authorized ACF leaders at the chapter, regional, or national level
  • Designated Elvanto administrators
  • Integrated platforms (e.g., Tithely Giving or WhatsApp) where applicable, with limited access

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

How We Keep It Safe

Elvanto is hosted by Tithely and uses secure encryption technologies (including SSL and AES-256). Internally, we:

  • Limit admin access using role-based permissions
  • Regularly audit user access
  • Require strong passwords and account protections

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and update your personal profile
  • Request removal from Elvanto
  • Opt out of communications at any time
  • Contact us regarding how your data is used

To make a request, email accounts@acfusa.org.

Data Retention

We retain member data for as long as you are active within the ACF community. Inactive profiles may be anonymized or deleted after a set period, subject to leadership review and any applicable legal obligations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be communicated via Elvanto announcements, email, or during regional events.
